Easy Butter Chicken
Step 1: Place yoghurt, garlic, ginger, cumin, coriander, garam masala and chilli powder in a glass or ceramic dish
Step 2: Add chicken. Stir to coat. Cover. Refrigerate for 2 hours
Step 3: Heat oil and butter in a heavy-based saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onion. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 to 4 minutes or until softened.
Step 4: Add chicken mixture to pan. Cook, stirring, for 5 minutes or until chicken just starts to change colour. 
Step 5: Add tomato puree and stock. Cover. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low. Simmer, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes or until chicken is tender and mixture has thickened slightly.
Step 6: Stir in cream. Simmer for a further 5 minutes or until heated through. Serve with steamed rice and coriander leaves.
